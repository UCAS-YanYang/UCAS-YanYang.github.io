How to estimate the quality of a paper roughly

Posted on 2023-06-25 In Research Skills
This note is about how to estimate the quality of a paper roughly when meeting with a unfamiliar journal.

​ There are two main journal ranking systems for journals: One is the “Journal Ranking” in Journal Citation Reports (JCR) by Clarivate Analytics; commonly referred to as “JCR Categories”. The other is the “Journal Classification Table” by the Chinese Academy of Sciences (CAS) Documentation and Information Center. Both of these systems are based on the impact factors (IF) of journals indexed in Clarivate Analytics’ Science Citation Index Expanded (SCIE) and Social Sciences Citation Index (SSCI)[1], [2]. For mathematic field, China Mathematical Society (CMS) Journal Classification may also help CMS Journal Classification.pdf (cms.org.cn).

​ As for how to search for the journals we are interested in, we may refer to methods in [3]. For some reviews or comments on corresponding journals, we can refer to https://www.letpub.com/ (Chinese version and English version are different a lot).
